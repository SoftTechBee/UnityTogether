White Paper ofUnity Together

Introduction

Unity Together is a unique financing model of crowd funding. It raises funds to fulfill the needs and dreams of people and utilizes human resources in this task. The purpose ofUnity Together is to eradicate poverty and unemployment from the world. It does not provide any financial assistance to make people economically strong. The algorithm ofUnity Together' smart contract promotes voluntary donation. As a result, the person receiving the donation becomes economically strong. In simple words, every individual included in theUnity Together community is always striving to make each other economically prosperous.

Blockchain-based Crowd Funding

This white paper presents a novel approach to crowd funding, leveraging blockchain technology to facilitate referral-based crowd funding. The evolution of blockchain has enabled crowd funding to transcend geographical boundaries, and smart contracts have rendered referral-based crowd funding transparent, secure, and autonomous. This paper elucidatesUnity Together' referral-based crowd funding paradigm, its operational framework, its implementation on the blockchain, and its potential advantages, offering a comprehensive overview of this innovative model.

Problems of Traditional Crowd Funding
  • Risk: The risk of project failure always remains, which also poses a threat to the fulfillment of the crowd funding purpose.
  • Lack of transparency: There may be a lack of transparency in the project's financial transactions and usage information.
  • Lack of reliability: Ineffective control of technology leads to a lack of trust among donors towards the project. Additionally, there is also a risk of fraud by the creator.
  • Limited access: In traditional projects, the reach of voluntary donors is limited to a specific area. This does not provide equal benefits to the recipients and also poses a risk to the project's success.

To address these issues,Unity Together has developed its crowd funding model based on blockchain and cryptocurrency.

Referral Mechanism ofUnity Together

InUnity Together, referral is a process where a donor encourages others in their network to also donate.Unity Together' smart contract provides donors with a unique referral link. When a new donor clicks on this referral link, they become a part of the community after completing some processes. Then, they continuously contribute to achievingUnity Together' objectives and making its community global. As a result, they receive donations that help fulfill their needs and dreams.

Blockchain Technology & Smart Contracts Ensure Security

Unity Together' referral program is executed via smart contracts on the blockchain, facilitating decentralization and global expansion. This pioneering program seeks to alleviate poverty and unemployment worldwide through voluntary donations, ultimately establishing a thriving global community.

"The referral program operates autonomously, leveraging blockchain technology and smart contracts, without human intervention or control."

Expectations from Donors

Unity Together has certain expectations from its donor community, including:

  • Strict adherence to the laws of their respective countries
  • Active contribution to the country's development and prosperity

Unity Together does not condone or support any actions that contravene these expectations.
